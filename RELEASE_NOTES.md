## App Store Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
AMEN is a social platform built specifically for the Christian community. Unlike general social apps, every feature in AMEN is designed around faith — to support your spiritual life and deepen your connection to other believers.

FAITH-FIRST SOCIAL FEED
Share what God is doing in your life. Post scripture, testimonies, reflections, photos, and prayer requests. Your feed surfaces the people and communities that share your values.

PRAYER WALL
Post prayer requests and pray for others in your network and your church. See when the community is praying for you — in real time.

CHURCH NOTES
Take notes during sermons with automatic scripture detection. Berean AI can instantly give you historical, theological, and cross-reference context for any passage you write down. Notes are private by default and shareable when you're ready.

FIND A CHURCH
Search for churches near you by denomination, style, and values. Get real service times, upcoming events, and an AI-powered visit readiness score so your first Sunday feels like home.

BEREAN AI
Ask questions about the Bible. Explore the historical background of any passage. Get denomination-aware theological context. Berean AI always cites its sources and tells you when it doesn't know — no hallucinated theology.

SPACES & COMMUNITIES
Build and join faith communities around what matters to you: Bible studies, prayer groups, ministry teams, worship circles, missions. Public or invite-only. Moderated and safe.

CREATOR TOOLS
Pastors, worship leaders, ministry creators, and theologians: publish sermons, devotionals, and Bible teaching directly to your community. Amen+ and CreatorPro subscriptions unlock advanced tools.

BUILT FOR YOUR WHOLE FAMILY
AMEN is designed from the ground up with safety at its center:
• All posts and media pass through automated moderation before reaching the feed
• Age-aware defaults give teens and children a different, safer experience automatically
• Full report and block controls on every post, profile, comment, and message
• No dark patterns, no algorithmic rage-bait, no ad tracking by default
• Full account deletion available in Settings at any time

SPIRITUAL WELLNESS
Synaptic Studio connects HealthKit data — heart rate, activity, sleep — with scripture-guided reflection. AMEN helps you see how your body and spirit are connected.

CHURCH CHEMISTRY
Find fellow believers from your existing contacts. See who in your phone is already part of the AMEN community, and connect around shared faith.

DAILY DIGEST
Start each morning with your personalized spiritual briefing: a scripture for the day, prayer requests from your community, church events coming up, and a Berean reflection.

This is AMEN. Faith community, built right.
```
