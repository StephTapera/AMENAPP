Contextual Awareness

Smart mode switching · Always private

AMEN can adapt to your context to serve you better — switching to Worship Mode at your church on Sunday, or Audio Bible while you're driving. Your data never leaves your device. No raw coordinates are ever stored.

📍
Location

Used only to detect nearby churches and switch to Worship Mode. Your location is NEVER stored. Context resolves to "First Baptist Church" — not coordinates. You can turn this off anytime.

🏃
Motion

Used only to detect if you're driving. Activates Drive Mode — feed hidden, audio controls shown. Motion data is processed on-device only. Never sent to servers. Turns off anytime.

📅
Calendar

Used to surface upcoming church events and service times. AMEN reads event titles only to recognize church events. Calendar data is never uploaded or shared. Optional — works without this.
