Amen Safety and Support

Amen provides reporting, blocking, moderation, and account support for user-generated content, communities, messages, media, and AI-assisted experiences.

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services first. Amen support channels are not emergency services.

Report Safety Concerns

Use the in-app report controls whenever possible. Reports can include abuse, harassment, threats, impersonation, scams, privacy violations, self-harm concerns, sexual exploitation, child-safety concerns, and unsafe content.

  • General support: support@amenapp.org
  • Trust and Safety: safety@amenapp.org
  • Privacy: privacy@amenapp.org
  • Legal requests: legal@amenapp.org

Child Safety and Exploitation

Reports involving suspected child sexual exploitation, grooming, online enticement, trafficking, sextortion, or sexualized minors are routed to restricted Trust and Safety workflows for trained review and evidence preservation.

Blocking and Account Controls

Amen includes block, hide, mute, report, delete account, and privacy controls where applicable. Some legal or moderation evidence may be retained when required by law, safety policy, or abuse-prevention obligations.

AI Safety

Amen reviews AI-assisted experiences for prompt injection, private-data leakage, unsafe spiritual authority, self-harm escalation, sexual exploitation, harassment, scams, and other abuse categories.
