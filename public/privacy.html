Amen Privacy Summary

This page summarizes privacy controls for App Store review and user support. Keep the full privacy policy and App Store privacy labels aligned with the production build.

Data Amen May Process

  • Account profile, authentication, and support data.
  • User-generated content such as posts, comments, prayer requests, notes, media, messages, reports, and communities.
  • AI requests, AI safety metadata, moderation metadata, and abuse-prevention signals.
  • Payment, subscription, creator, or donation data when paid features are enabled.
  • Diagnostics, analytics, device, App Check, and security logs as configured in production.

User Controls

Users can access privacy settings, clear supported AI history, report content or users, block users, and delete their account in-app. Some safety, legal, and moderation records may be retained where required.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to privacy@amenapp.org. Safety issues can be sent to Amen Safety and Support.
