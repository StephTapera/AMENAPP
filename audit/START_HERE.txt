================================================================================
ARCHITECTURE VERDICT
================================================================================

✓ GOOD:
  - Server-side enforcement (client checks advisory)
  - Fail-open safeguards (never silently approve)
  - Sacred/monitored separation (encryption for adults)
  - Multi-layer defense (banned terms → Perspective → Vision)
  - Crisis never blocked (always delivers + resources)

✗ NEEDS WORK:
  - 4+ moderation entry points (too complex)
  - Privacy gaps (DM & comment disclosure)
  - No appeal mechanism (user fairness)
  - Async comment moderation (silent deletion)
  - Shadow-ban possible (deceptive)

OVERALL: Production-ready with critical fixes needed before scaling.
