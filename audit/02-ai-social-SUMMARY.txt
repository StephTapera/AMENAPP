================================================================================
LEGAL/COMPLIANCE NOTES
================================================================================

COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):
  ⚠ Monitored channels (minors) lack required parental/minor notification
  ✓ Data practices disclosed in privacy policy (recommend link in composer)

GDPR (EU):
  ✓ Users can request access to moderation decisions (recommend appeal form)
  ✓ Right to erasure: not discussed (archive moderation logs separately)

FTC Act (Unfair/Deceptive Practices):
  ⚠ Shadow-banning is potentially deceptive (recommend removing)
  ✓ Moderation transparency: partial (gap: no user notification of decisions)

Consumer Rights (California, etc.):
  ⚠ DM disclosure: consumers should know if content is sent to third-party AI
  ⚠ Appeal: consumers should have mechanism to challenge moderation
