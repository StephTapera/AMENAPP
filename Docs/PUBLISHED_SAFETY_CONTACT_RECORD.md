# Amen Published Safety and Contact Record

This record proves that public support and safety contact information is live before App Store submission.

## Machine-Checked Markers

```text
SAFETY_CONTACT_PAGE_LIVE=false
SAFETY_CONTACT_URL=
SAFETY_CONTACT_OWNER=
SAFETY_CONTACT_REVIEW_DATE=
```

## Required Page Content

The published page must include:

- A general support contact.
- A Trust & Safety contact or report channel.
- Child-safety and exploitation escalation language.
- Instructions for reporting abuse, harassment, impersonation, scams, threats, sexual exploitation, child-safety concerns, and self-harm concerns.
- A privacy contact.
- A law-enforcement/legal request channel or process.
- Expected response paths for urgent safety issues.

## Required Final Checks

- [ ] URL is public without login.
- [ ] URL is listed in App Store metadata, app support links, and privacy policy where applicable.
- [ ] Contact inbox/workflow is monitored.
- [ ] Urgent child-safety and exploitation reports route to trained reviewers.
